THE DANGER CLIFFS STORY

HOW IT STARTED

Danger Cliffs was born on the slopes of Mt. Hood years ago. Through years of scouring trail maps searching for new terrain to be skied, it stared back at us... "Danger Cliffs". An obscure region of the map filled with dangerous terrain, labeled as a "closed area".
Then, during one cold snowy night of skiing we realized it wasn’t a warning of a “no-go”; but an obvious invitation of a “what if”!

Image Source: Skibowl.com

WHAT IT BECAME

From then on is history – we found that the Danger Cliffs invitation can be applied to any and all things; wherever you are going, whatever you are doing – Live the Life!!

The Danger Cliffs logo has become a territory marker for this community. A way to mark terrain that you have conquered, a cool spot you find, or to add to the sticker wall at your favorite bar.

"LIVE THE LIFE"

Danger Cliffs should be a reminder to live the kind of life that you want to live, whatever that may be! You should push the boundaries that are set for you, and take risks in an effort to better yourself both as a person, and as a skier, hiker, biker, climber, surfer, runner, or whatever you like to do!

Use the Danger Cliffs logo to mark your territory, but also to remind others to "Life the Life" that makes them better. Use our message to spread positivity and motivation throughout all communities, and never stop living the life!

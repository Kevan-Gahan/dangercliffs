Welcome! If you found this website, it is likely because you saw our sticker somewhere. This is your official invitation to our community of outdoor enthusiasts. Welcome to Danger Cliffs! You are now one of us.

Danger Cliffs is a lifestyle, a territory marker, a brand... Really, Danger Cliffs can be whatever you need it to be. We are a small but growing group of outdoorsy people rooted near Mt. Hood, Oregon - but our logo is found all over the world. We are avid skiers, hikers, bikers, runners, climbers, surfers, surf-foilers, and lovers of outdoor drinking! You name it, we do it... or at least try!

Danger Cliffs is a constant reminder that you should always push the boundaries that are set for you. Face the danger. Go off the groomed path, climb the peak, duck the rope, go the extra mile, ride the swell, finish the can... Or do them all together! There is no limit to Danger Cliffs because there is no limit to the progress and growth that can only be found outside of YOUR comfort zone.

Everyone is welcome! Join the Danger Cliffs movement, set goals for yourself, meet those goals, and set new ones. Start living the life today!
